🍽️ **Restaurant Data Analysis Project**


📌 **Overview**


This project analyzes a restaurant dataset to extract meaningful insights about cuisines, ratings, pricing, and customer behavior.
It uses Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn, Folium) for data analysis and visualization.


🎯 **Objectives**


Identify top cuisines and their popularity

Analyze restaurant ratings across cities

Study price range distribution

Explore online delivery and table booking trends

Detect restaurant chains and analyze their performance

Visualize restaurant locations on maps

Identify clusters and patterns in restaurant distribution

Analyze relationships between:

Votes and ratings

Price range and services


🛠️**Technologies Used**


Python 🐍

Pandas

NumPy

Matplotlib

Scikit-learn

Folium (for maps)


📂 **Dataset**


The dataset contains:

Restaurant Name

City

Cuisines

Aggregate Rating

Votes

Price Range

Online Delivery

Table Booking

Latitude & Longitude



📊 **Key Analysis Performed**


1. Top Cuisines
Identified most common cuisines
Calculated their percentage distribution

2. City-wise Ratings
Computed average ratings for each city

3. Price Range Distribution
Visualized using bar charts

4. Online Delivery Analysis
Percentage of restaurants offering delivery
Compared ratings (with vs without delivery)

5. Rating Distribution
Identified most common rating range

6. Votes Analysis
Average votes per restaurant
Relationship between votes and ratings

7. Cuisine Combinations
Found popular cuisine combinations

8. Location Analysis
Plotted restaurants on maps using latitude & longitude

9. Clustering
Used K-Means to identify restaurant clusters

10. Restaurant Chains
Identified chains based on repeated names
Analyzed:
Average ratings
Popularity (votes)
Number of outlets

11. Services Analysis
Relationship between price range and:
Online delivery
Table booking


📈 **Key Insights**


⭐ Most restaurants fall in the 3–4 rating range

🍜 North Indian & Chinese are most common cuisines

🚚 Lower-priced restaurants → more online delivery

🏨 Higher-priced restaurants → more table booking

👍 Weak positive correlation between votes and ratings

📍 Restaurants are clustered in high-demand urban areas
